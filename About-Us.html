About Our Story

For over 25 years, we've been transforming spaces with premium natural stone, combining traditional and modern techniques to create timeless beauty.To provide exceptional beauty in their's Era.

Our Journey

Founded in 1998 by master Awan Group, Awan Marble & Granite began as a small family business with a mission to bring the natural beauty of stone into people's homes and businesses.

What started as a modest workshop has grown into one of the regions most trusted stone fabrication and installation companies. We've completed more than 1,000 projects, from residential kitchens to large commercial spaces.

Today, we continue to honor our founding principles of quality and customer satisfaction by introducing new technologies and techniques that allow us to serve our clients better than ever.
